Introit
-------

Requiem aeternam habebimus omnes.				We shall all have eternal rest.
Memorias et vivet in aeternum.					Memories will live forever.
Lux perpetua lucebit eis in aeternum.			A perpetual light will shine on them forever.

Prepare ourselves to celebrate					Para nos ad celebrandum
the life of a man.  							Vita hominis.
-A good man.									Vir bonus.

Lacrimosa
---------
Tears now and tomorrow.							Lacrimosa nunc et cras.
We will cry forever.							Aetermum flebimus.
Though we show it not 							Etsi non ostendimus.
Tears.											Lacrimae.

Only his ashes remain.							Tantum cineres manent.
Cold comfort.									Frigus consolatio.

I will forget some day,							Ego obliviscar aliquando,
I'm going to forget,							Ego obliviscar ando.
Bitter, bitter, the bitterness, sadness			Amarum, amarum, in amarum, tristia.

But we will not stop crying.					Sed flere non desinemus.
But I will not stop crying.						Sed non desinam flere.

I don't want to forget.							Nolo oblivisci.
We don't want to forget.						Oblivisci nolumus.

until our death									usque ad mortem nostram

we will weep until we die 						flebimus donec moriamur

Tears are blinding me.							Lacrimae me excaecant.

We can't see through the tears.					Ultra lacrimas videre non possumus.

But now I see nothing through tears.

I cannot see through (my) tears.

raining											pluit

Tears rain on ashes.							lacrimis pluat in cinere

We will weep until we die.						Flebimus donec moriamur.

We weep over ashes until our time comes.		
All will die.
Death is everywhere.
