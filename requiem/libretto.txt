Note: the audience words start with a hyphen

Greeting
--------

In the name of Oscar, Cecil, and Mary.			In nomine Oscar, Cecilius et Maria.
-So be it.										Amen.

Be with your brothers,							Esto fratribus tuis,
-And they with you.								Et apud te.

Prepare ourselves to celebrate					Para nos ad celebrandum
the life of a man.  							Vita hominis.
A good man.										Vir bonus.
